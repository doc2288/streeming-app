## 2026-04-16 - Form Accessibility and Native Validation
**Learning:** Pairing component-level form validation with native HTML5 validation attributes (`required`, `minLength`, etc.) and explicit `htmlFor`/`id` linking is crucial for screen reader support and keyboard accessibility.
**Action:** Always link `<label>` and `<input>` elements using `htmlFor` and `id`, and utilize native HTML5 validation attributes to provide standard, accessible validation feedback before custom component logic runs.
